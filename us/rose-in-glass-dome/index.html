A GIFT THAT WILL BE REMEMBERED FOR A LONG TIME

Live and fresh cut flowers are considered to be a universal gift—everyone likes them regardless of age, hobbies or social status, and their impeccable attractive appearance gives aesthetic pleasure. However, typical fresh flowers from the store can not maintain their attractive appearance for long. The youth and freshness of a flower bud disappear, leaving no trace after a week from the moment they were cut.

That is why we use roses that have been processed with stabilization, which allows them to stay fresh for such a long time. Thus, they can be called "a forever rose in a glass dome."
