Forever rose is a gift which worth a thousand words

A forever blooming rose in a glass dome would be an amazing idea for a gift to present in a big event or without a special reason for a loved one. Perfectly made, high-quality gift can suit a different age category of women, and even high status men. There is no need for a special reason to present such a gift; it is enough to have tender feeling to a person.

This is a real rose

We use a rose, which has absolutely no difference from the freshly cut flower. The queen of flowers in glass dome is alive; you can gently touch it and see it for yourself. The rose remains alive for so long due to a special method of stabilization.

Undeniable quality

The gift created by our company has an excellent quality as we pay special attention to it. Elite roses are grown on our plantations in Ecuador and Spain. Therefore, people prefer Nota & Belle company that has proven itself in the best way and it is always in charge of the products quality. The company main goal is the customers’ satisfaction with their gift choice.

Forever rose maintenance

Rose in a glass dome maintenance does not require any special care. Although some recommendations should be followed:
1. Try to avoid sudden temperature changes
2. Do not place the rose under the direct sun
3. Do not water it.
Forever rose lifetime won’t change even without a glass dome.

Personalized forever rose

You have a great opportunity to make this amazing gift very special and original by adding a gift box or ordering personalized engraving for it. Such a gift can cause the warmest feelings to a person who receives it, admire him by the individuality of an original engraving, and remain in his memory for a long time.

Gift box

A forever blooming rose gift box is available in white and black colours. When opening the gift box because of its originality causes a "WOW" effect. Also we provide a simpler version for free.

Engraving

Forever living rose with an engraving is a great thing very solemn situation. You can order this option with an engraving using a beautiful gold colour metal plate. When choosing a phrase or a word, try to bring a special meaning to it so as it would accurately affect a person soul and remain in a memory for a long time.

Free envelope with a note
